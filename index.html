Think very carefully about this. In a country with over 1.8 million graduates anually, are there enough opportunities out there? Are therw the kinds of opportunities that you may want? What makes you standout in the labour market?

Spartan Graduate is a series of master classes put together by AIESEC Enugu for students of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka. Its an opportunity for you to upscale your skillset and amplify your chances of landing your perfect job when you graduate.

Its time to get you refined, rewired and refueled for life after school. Learn High Income, High Demand skills for your future career path. Skillsets such as Business Development, Content Creation, Data Science, Design, Finance, Photography and Visual Storytelling, Product Management, and Technology - Software Development.
